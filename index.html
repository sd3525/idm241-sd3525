IDM 241 Final Build
Microinteraction:

GLF 2.0

Microinteraction Structure:
Triggers:
Hover on and off of card
Hover on and off of little sneaker
Click on little sneaker
Hover on and off of cart
Click on cart
Rules:
HOVER ON CARD
Cursor changes from pointer to finger
2px border, glf 2.0, and cart fades in
HOVER ON SMALL SNEAKER
1px border fades in
HOVER ON SMALL CART
Cart gets bigger
HOVER OFF CARD
Cursor changes from finger to pointer
2px border, glf 2.0, and cart fades out
HOVER OFF SMALL SNEAKER
1px border fades out
HOVER OFF SMALL CART
Cart goes back to normal size
CLICK ON SMALL SNEAKER
Large sneaker image fades to a new sneaker image
CLICK ON CART
Number 1 pops up over the cart
